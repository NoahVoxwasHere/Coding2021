About Me

Noah Vox/Noah Kot-Vox is an 18 year old, Trans-Masculine, artist from Canada, working to become a fulltime creator and animator. Noah graduated from highschool in 2020 in th ethick of COVID-19, which stunted his ability to attend post-secondary as quickly as he had wanted. In Fall of 2021, Noah officially started schooling at the Lethbridge College under the MMP (the Multimedia Production) course. Noah plans to use what he learns in the course to further his career into the art and animation industry.

When he was 15, Noah came out as Transgender to friends in highchool, Genderfluid, to be exact. From 15 to 18, life has had its struggles under the Trans umbrella, but there is no better feeling than being true to yourself and fighting for what you know is right. On June 4, 2021, Noah started testosterone and began officially moving towards his transitioning goals. On September 30, Noah has an appointment set with a surgeon in Calgary to meet and have a consultation for top surgery, also known as breast removal surgery.

Noah’s hobbies include reading, and writing, both skills which have helped Noah become a vastly creative man. In late 2020, Noah started production and writing of his first creative project “Alixe,” an Alice in Wonderland retelling of sorts that is to be written and illustrated by Noah. Inspiration for Noah’s projects stem from music, cartoons, TV-shows, movies, and friends.

After graduating from the Multimedia course, Noah plans to complete production of “Alixe,” as well as continue development for many many other projects. The most prevalent projects all tend to be comic, or graphic novel projects, but one in the near future is planned out to be in animation, or animatic. With the skills in video editing, sound mixing, and animation Noah hopes to apply all these skills in future projects.
